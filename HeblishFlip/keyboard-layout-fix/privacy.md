# Privacy Policy — Heblish

**Last updated:** 2026-07-21

## Summary

Heblish converts text you typed with the wrong keyboard layout (Hebrew ↔ English) **entirely on your device**, inside the web page you are editing.

## Data collection

- We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data or typed text.
- We do **not** use analytics, advertising, or tracking.
- Converted text never leaves your browser tab.
- There are no external network requests for conversion.

## Local usage stats (optional display)

The popup may show how many times you used the shortcut, how many characters were fixed, and an estimated time saved. These numbers are stored **only in your browser** (`chrome.storage.local` / `browser.storage.local`) on your computer. They are never sent to us or any server.

## Optional support link

The extension popup may open **Buy Me a Coffee** in a new tab only when **you** click the support button. No payment data passes through the extension.

## Optional keyboard helper (Windows, advanced)

Users who separately install the optional native messaging helper allow the extension to request a local Windows keyboard layout switch. That helper runs only on your PC and does not send text to the internet.

## Permissions

- **http / https access:** read and replace text in the focused field when you press the shortcut.
- **scripting:** inject the converter if a tab opened before install.
- **storage:** save local usage stats on your device only (see above).
- **nativeMessaging:** only for users who opt in to the optional Windows keyboard helper.

## Contact

Store reviews or your Buy Me a Coffee page for feedback.
